## Part 2 — What Is ClientBloom?

### 2.1 The Simple Answer

ClientBloom is a client intelligence platform built for service-based businesses. It helps you understand what your clients are actually thinking, feeling, and needing so your team can deliver an exceptional experience and keep clients longer.

Most service businesses have a blind spot: they know what work they delivered, but they have no clear picture of how the client actually feels about the relationship. ClientBloom fixes that blind spot.

### 2.2 The Two Products Under the ClientBloom Brand

**Scout** (`scout.clientbloom.ai`)
Scout is the lead intelligence tool. It helps service businesses and agencies find the right prospects by surfacing LinkedIn-based relationship intelligence. Scout is where the relationship starts — find the right person, understand the context, begin building a meaningful connection.

**ClientBloom** (`clientbloom.ai`)
ClientBloom is the client retention and intelligence platform. Once you have won a client through Scout or any other channel, ClientBloom helps you keep them. It monitors client health, flags at-risk relationships before they become a problem, and tells your team exactly what to do about it.

**How They Connect:** Scout finds the relationship. ClientBloom protects it. They are designed to work in sequence, but each product also stands on its own.

### 2.3 Who ClientBloom Is For

ClientBloom is built for small to mid-sized service businesses, typically with 10 to 100 team members and revenue between $500K and $5M. Businesses that get the most value from it include:

- Marketing and creative agencies
- Business consultants and coaching firms
- Financial services providers
- Health and wellness businesses
- Legal services
- IT and managed service providers

The common thread: these businesses live and die by long-term client relationships. One lost client does not just cost one month of revenue — it costs the entire lifetime value of that relationship.

### 2.4 The Core Problem ClientBloom Solves

Most service businesses manage client relationships reactively. A client goes quiet, a payment is late, a project stalls — and by the time anyone notices, the damage is already done. The client has mentally checked out weeks before they send the cancellation email.

ClientBloom gives you the signal before you would ever notice it on your own. It watches data across every touchpoint — billing interactions, communication patterns, and engagement signals — and tells you what is actually happening in the relationship right now.

## Part 3 — ClientBloom Platform Features

### 3.1 The Client Retention Score (CRS)

The Client Retention Score is the centerpiece of the platform — a proprietary AI-powered algorithm that condenses every signal from a client relationship into a single score from 0 to 10.

| Range | Status | Meaning |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 7.0–10.0 | 🟢 Green | Healthy relationship. May have expansion opportunities. |
| 4.0–6.99 | 🟡 Yellow | Early warning. Something needs attention before it becomes a problem. |
| 0–3.99 | 🔴 Red | High risk. Requires immediate action. |

The score pulls from multiple data sources: AI-analyzed call quality, billing status, onboarding progress, communication frequency, community engagement, and attendance patterns.

**Important context:** Green does not mean ignore. Red does not always mean the client is leaving. The CRS is a starting point for a human conversation, not a replacement for one.

### 3.2 The BI Agent (Business Intelligence Agent)

The BI Agent is the autonomous intelligence layer inside ClientBloom. Every morning, it runs an analysis across the entire client base and delivers a prioritized briefing. It does not just show data — it tells you which client to call today, what is happening in that relationship, and what to do about it.

The BI Agent learns three things about your business:
1. Your industry, service model, client types, and team structure — so recommendations are specific to how you actually operate
2. Each individual client's relationship patterns over time — so it knows what "normal" looks like and can flag when something shifts
3. Cross-client patterns that improve prediction accuracy the longer you use the platform

Briefings are delivered via email or Slack. No dashboard to interpret — the agent does the interpretation and hands you the actions.

### 3.3 Client Health Dashboard

The dashboard gives a real-time view of every client relationship in the business. Color-coded by health status, it shows onboarding progress, engagement trends, revenue at risk, and communication history across every account.

### 3.4 New Client Launch Tracker

The Launch Tracker monitors every new client through their onboarding milestones. Research shows that clients who do not complete onboarding properly are significantly more likely to churn early. The tracker flags any client falling behind before the relationship has had a chance to take hold.

### 3.5 Integrations

ClientBloom connects to the tools businesses already use. Native integrations include: Slack, GoHighLevel (GHL), Circle, Zoom, and Zapier (which opens access to 2,000+ additional apps). Additional integrations including HubSpot are in development.

ClientBloom is designed to be additive to an existing tech stack, not a replacement for it. No data migration required.

### 3.6 Reports and Analytics

ClientBloom generates reports on retention trends, engagement patterns, revenue at risk, churn forecasting, and team performance. Reports can be shared with leadership or team members in seconds.

## Part 4 — How Scout Works and How the Products Connect

### 4.1 What Scout Does

Scout is a LinkedIn relationship intelligence tool. It helps businesses identify the right prospects by surfacing relevant posts and contact context based on ideal customer criteria. Users monitor ICP profiles and keyword signals to find people worth building a relationship with — before competitors do.

Scout handles the front end. Once that relationship is established and a client is on board, ClientBloom takes over — monitoring health over time, flagging when something needs attention, and helping the team consistently deliver an experience worth staying for.

No. ClientBloom is built specifically for service businesses — agencies, consultants, coaches, and service providers whose clients are people, not user accounts.

The autonomous intelligence layer inside ClientBloom. Each morning it analyzes your entire client base and delivers a prioritized briefing to your inbox or Slack: which client needs attention, what is happening, and what specific action to take.

**What is the Client Retention Score?**
A proprietary AI-powered algorithm that scores each client from 0 to 10 based on call quality, billing status, communication frequency, onboarding progress, and community engagement. Green = healthy, Yellow = early warning, Red = at risk.

**Does ClientBloom share my data with other businesses?**
No. Each ClientBloom workspace is isolated. The platform uses your business's data to serve your business only.
